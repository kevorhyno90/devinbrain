Download a beautifully formatted PDF report of all your plans, with statistics, category breakdown, completion rates, and a full task list. Perfect for weekly reviews or sharing with mentors/family.

📚 How to Use BrainJet

🌅 BrainJet is your personal life command center. Organize plans across 3 categories:

  • 🧠 Mind — spiritual reflection, learning, mental wellness
  • 🌱 Farm — crops, livestock, equipment, harvests
  • ⚡ Task — family time, errands, work, household
  • 🏋️ Fitness — workouts, health, diet
  • 💰 Finance — budgeting, bills, investments

⚡ Features:

  • Works 100% offline — your data stays on your device
  • Strict date system with multi-stage reminders (before, due, overdue)
  • Install as native app — find it on your home screen
  • Free AI assistant (Jet) — chat anytime for help
  • Download PDF reports anytime for printing or sharing
  • Export/import JSON backups for safekeeping
⚙️ Preferences
Switch between light and dark mode
When online, use more powerful AI (free, no signup)
Play sound on reminders & task completion
💾 Data Management

All data is stored locally on your device using IndexedDB. Nothing is sent to any server. Back up regularly to keep your data safe across devices.
